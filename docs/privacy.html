Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-10-19 • Last updated: 2025-10-19

This Privacy Policy explains how The Cookout (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: email, display name, profile photo (from Firebase or third-party OAuth such as TikTok).
  • Authentication data: tokens and identifiers necessary to sign you in.
  • Usage data: app interactions, device info (general, non-sensitive) for reliability and security.
  • User content: any text, images, or other content you submit.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and secure account login (Firebase Authentication, TikTok Login).
  • Operate features, sync data (Firebase Database/Storage), and improve the App.
  • Prevent abuse, troubleshoot, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing

We share information with service providers only as needed to operate the App (e.g., Google Firebase, TikTok for login). We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Retention

We keep data while your account is active and as needed for the purposes above. You may request deletion; see “Your Choices”.

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can access/update profile data in-app.
  • To delete your account or request data export, email support@thecookout.app.
  • You may revoke TikTok permissions via your TikTok settings.

6. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). If we learn we collected such data, we will delete it.

7. Security

We use reasonable safeguards (e.g., Firebase Security Rules) to protect data, but no method is 100% secure.

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed in locations where our providers operate (e.g., the United States).

9. Changes

We may update this Policy. We’ll post the new version here and update the “Effective” date.
